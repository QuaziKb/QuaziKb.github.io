Converging Diverging Rocket Nozzle


While taking a course on compressible fluid dynamics, we kept revisiting nozzle flow as an interesting example. This is seemingly because nozzle flow captures 1D compressible fluid dynamic problems quite well, and thus works well as an illustrative example.
